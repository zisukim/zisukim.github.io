Which is worse - failing or never trying?

Veröffentlicht am 2019-04-28 09:25 | in Languages | Kommentare:

This question is from 87 Self-Reflection Questions for Introspection.

I think that nothing is worse than never trying for my future.

I’ve had time without any challenge.
I was afraid to fail and concerned that all the time that I tried to accomplish it disappeared.
I did only the work that is easy to succeed or requires less time.
I thought I could develop myself while saving time efficiently.
I spent tons of time to find a way to reach my goal more easily and quickly.
I found that I didn’t try to do and complete anything.

I’ve struggled a variety of things since then. 👊
Not growing is more frightening than failure.

라이브 아카데미 이유와 목적 말하기

Veröffentlicht am 2019-04-27 16:17 | in Languages | Kommentare:

Expressions

because

  • 대부분의 경우에 사용할 수 있음.

since

  • 서로 다 아는, 상대방이 알 고 있을 거라고 예상하는 사실
  • 이유라기 보다는 조건

Since next week is a holiday week, I figured I should take care of it before then.

due to

  • ~로 인해
  • 직결되는 원인과 결과(반응..?)

There’s some damage around the house due to the hurricane last week.

in order to

  • (in order) to 생략 가능
  • in order for someone to ~ : 주어랑 다른 대상일 경우 주어를 넣어주셈

I turned on my computer (in order) to check my email.
You need to give me all the information in order for me to help you.

so that

  • 목적
  • 하도록, 하게

Let me know where you’re hanging out so that I can join you guys.
I gave her some advice so that she could make a good decision.
He took me the school by his car so that I could attend a lecture.

ETC

I figured I should
해야될 것 같다.

라이브 아카데미 deserve

Veröffentlicht am 2019-04-23 13:15 | in Languages | Kommentare:

Expressions

I worked really hard semester,
나는 이번 학기에 정말 열심히 했어.
I think I deserve a little break.
= I think I deserve to take a little break.
좀 쉬어도 될 것 같아. (그럴 만한 자격이 있다. 그 것을 벌었다.)

He is being so unreasonable.
그 사람이 아는 것은 말도 안돼.
You don’t deserve that kind of treatment.
= You don’t deserve to be treated like that way.
널 그렇게 취급하면 안되지.

Some people say she deserves a second chance.
But I honestly think she deserves to be put in the jail.

ETC

get what one deserves
I got what I deserved.
나는 마땅한 대가를 치루었어. (부정적인..?)

라이브 아카데미 생각이 나다 - 생각이 들다 - 생각해내다

Veröffentlicht am 2019-04-18 05:40 | in Languages | Kommentare:

Expressions

Remember
Do you remember the name of that place?
혹시 거기 이름 생각나?

I figured I should
I figured we should go to a nice place.
좋은 데로 가야겠다는 생각이 들었는데
I figured I should take him to a nice place.

Think of
I couldn’t really think of anywhere/anyplace.
딱히 생각나는 데가 없어서
I can think of a few places.
생각나는 데가 몇 군데 있어.

Come up with

  • 좀 더 창의적인 생각 과정을 그릴 때 사용
    He came up with a really great idea.

ETC

be called

  • 불리는
  • Do you remember what that place was called?

혹시

  • happen to
    • Do you happen to remember it?
  • by any chance
    • Do you by any chance remember it?

you mean

  • ~ 말이지.
  • You mean that seafood restaurant!

-something

  • 뭐뭐
  • I think It was “Deep Blue -something-“.
