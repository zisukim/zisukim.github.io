핵심은 일관성이다.

전체적인 내용은 어마무시한 거를 단기간에 똿 완성하려 하거나
특정한 기간에만 열심히 달리려고 하지말고, 평소에 조금씩 꾸준히 하라는 것임.
Input(Listening, Speaking)은 너를 유창하게 만들지 않으므로 꼭 Output(Speaking, Writing)을 함께 해줘야 함.

아티엔 바나나 후치수식

Posted on 2019-05-11 13:01 | In Languages | Comments:

후치 수식

  • 한국어는 전치 수식을 사용한다.
  • 영어는 전치 수식과 후치 수식 둘 다 사용한다.
    • 수식하는 말이 길어지면 후치 수식을 사용해준다.

전명구

  • A book in my bag.
  • A new shop in New York has many customers.
  • A new cafe over the road opened last week.
  • I used to learn English watching videos on Youtube.
  • Is the blue cup beside your labtop yours?

형용사구

  • A girl mad at me

분사 ing/pp

  • The man wearing blue jacket will guide you to the entrance.
  • I fell asleep with TV turned on.
  • He sat there with his eyes closed.

to 부정사의 형용사적 용법

  • Can I have somthing to drink?
    • something to ~ v
    • something adj
  • I need somthing to read on my way.
  • This is the letter to return her.
  • He is getting ready for some snacks to eat together.

관계대명사절

  • A girl who has blond hair and wears a dress.
  • That guy who is ordering in front of counter is my friend.
  • I ordered a cup of coffee which is written on the board.
  • He plans to create an application that helps him to make more money.

관계부사절

  • I don’t know exact time when we go to lunch.
  • He just told me the reason why he likes a pork cutlet.
  • This is the store where I bought my glasses.

동격절

  • I heard about the news that your ex-boyfriend is getting married soon.
  • I found a piece of evidence that he ate my waffle secretly.
  • It was a common belief that a mad man exists in anywhere.

라이브 아카데미 다양한

Posted on 2019-05-11 12:33 | In Languages | Comments:

Expressions

보통 various, a variety of, diverse를 쓰지만..

many different

  • 개수
  • There were many different menus.

many different kinds of

  • 종류
  • There were many different kinds of menus.

all kinds(sort) of

  • 온갖 것들
  • 모든 종류의 것들
  • 별것 다
  • There were all sort of stuff.

많 ~~~ 은 (빈도수)

  • so various (X)
  • a wide variety/vast of
  • sooooooooooo many different kinds of
    • There were so many different types of people.
