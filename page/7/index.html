Which is worse - failing or never trying?

Veröffentlicht am 2019-04-28 09:25 | in Languages | Kommentare:

This question is from 87 Self-Reflection Questions for Introspection.

I think that nothing is worse than never trying for my future.

I’ve had time without any challenge.
I was afraid to fail and concerned that all the time that I tried to accomplish it disappeared.
I did only the work that is easy to succeed or requires less time.
I thought I could develop myself while saving time efficiently.
I spent tons of time to find a way to reach my goal more easily and quickly.
I found that I didn’t try to do and complete anything.

I’ve struggled a variety of things since then. 👊
Not growing is more frightening than failure.
